# Consumer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Retail Customer Analytics for Business Growth and Customer Engagement

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing consumer shopping behavior for a leading retail company to uncover patterns in customer purchasing decisions, improve customer engagement, and optimize business strategies.

The analysis explores how factors such as demographics, discounts, customer reviews, seasonal trends, payment preferences, and sales channels (online vs offline) influence customer purchases and loyalty.

The project combines:
- Python for data cleaning and preprocessing
- SQL for business analysis and querying
- Power BI for dashboard visualization
- Data analytics techniques for actionable business insights

---

## Business Problem Statement

A retail company aims to better understand customer shopping behavior in order to:
- Improve sales performance
- Increase customer satisfaction
- Enhance customer loyalty
- Optimize marketing strategies
- Identify key purchase drivers

### Core Business Question

> “How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?”

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify high-performing product categories
- Understand customer demographics
- Study online vs offline shopping trends
- Analyze the impact of discounts and reviews
- Discover repeat purchase patterns
- Generate business recommendations using data insights
